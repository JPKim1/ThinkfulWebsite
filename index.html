Poem

All Melody

Eyes closed,

the harmony consists of two or three tones.

Echos of a motorcar from opposite ends,

a constant timbre, unexpectedly.

Bass tones like a balloon inflating,

a balloon without punctures,

and limitless capacity.

Rare is the third tone,

the deepest and most resonant.

A plane that reverberates like a giant propeller,

but a plane without propellers.

There is no unison, each note

a different movement

but of the same sonata.
